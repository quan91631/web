BRILLANT'S CORE VALUES

At Brillant, we always uphold 5 Core Values ​​so that every step is more solid and valuable.

1. Customer centric

Putting customer satisfaction as the number 1 priority in every thought and action.

2. Ownership & Autonomy

Owning the goals of the company, team, and individual. Proactively seeking resources and solutions. Thinking and acting for the best interests of the organization.

3. Integrity

Being honest about money, goods, and assets. Doing the right thing, doing enough according to the design. Keeping promises to customers, colleagues, and partners. When you have tried your best but see a risk of not being able to keep your word, immediately inform relevant people, find out the impact, clean up the consequences, and make a new promise.

4. Growth mindset - Progress

Continuously learning and self-improving. Willing to try new methods, accept failure and move on. Always see the positive intention in every situation.

5. Good Relationship - Good Relationship

Generous, willing to give and help people. Put yourself in the speaker's shoes to listen fully and without prejudice. Willing to contribute and receive contributions in a timely, direct and constructive manner. Always acknowledge achievements or efforts even if they have not yet been achieved

History

1. Origin: The birth of the brand

Brillant was founded in 1988, in Ho Chi Minh City. The founder, Daiki Do, started his business with a strong passion for fashion and a desire to bring revolutionary designs to the industry. Initially, the brand focused on pants, shirts, handbags, etc. Standing out with its exquisite craftsmanship and unique design style. The first products quickly attracted the attention of the upper class and aristocracy.

2. Development and expansion

In the first decade, Brillant quickly built a reputation for iconic products such as shirts and pants. These products not only created trends but also affirmed the brand's position in the high-end fashion industry. Thanks to the support of connoisseurs and famous stars, the brand began to expand its market internationally, from Europe to America, Asia and other regions. In the 20s, Brillant became a symbol of luxury and global class.

3. Symbols and heritage

The elements that make up the brand's name include: Design style: A combination of classic and modern, minimalist and luxurious. Material: Using high-quality materials such as genuine leather, silk, cashmere... Brand symbol: B

4. Challenges and innovation

In the 2000s, the brand faced challenges from the market, including fierce competition and changing consumer tastes. To adapt, Brillant innovated by collaborating with famous designers, developing sustainable products, and applying technology to design. One of the major turning points was the arrival of John, who brought a breath of fresh air, helping the brand return to the top.

5. Current position

Today, Brillant is one of the leading icons of the luxury fashion industry, with a network of stores spanning the world. The combination of tradition and modernity, along with a commitment to quality and creativity, has helped Brillant maintain its leading position in the hearts of connoisseurs
